OPIS

Finance with a snap: the simplest budgeting solution for busy people

What is Opis?

We are your one-stop solution to budgeting. By photographing a receipt or bank statement, users can see how their budget fares with fun, sharable data visualizations such as pie charts or line graphs. Working on a group project that involves reimbursements? Simply share your project, and everyone on your team will have access to the same budget. No credit card information necessary.

Financing? What a pain.

Do you find it hard to keep your finances in check when your cash seems to disappear with a swipe of a card? Lacking the time to jot down every single purchase you bought this week? Struggling with the consequences? You're in good company- by the time of graduation, the average American student will have racked up $47k in debt.

We got you covered.

Instead of remembering to record every little thing you bought on your spreadsheet and potentially making calculation mistakes along the way, Opis makes budgeting fun and simple. After taking a photo of your receipt, Opis will read and upload the information onto pie charts and line graphs. By visualizing the budgeting process, users can now see exactly how much pocket money they have left for pizza this weekend by spending just a second glancing at that pie chart!

Opis: Budgeting Rediscovered

With us, know that we cannot record your credit card information. And why would we want to? Happy cusomters means happy Opis.

Steps to Opis:

1. Create an account

2. Create a new project and enter in your spending goal.

3. Choose the right data visualization for you.

4. Take a photo of your receipt

5.Confirm the amount

6. That's it!
